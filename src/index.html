Journeys was born from the belief that people with developmental disabilities deserve access to the same resources and opportunities that are available to the non-disabled community.

Important to this idea is the right to live in a home of oneâ€™s own.

Journeys Supported Living Services assists developmentally disabled adults to locate, secure, and maintain affordable housing. We provide a network of support to ensure that the needs of the individuals we serve are foremost in the process.
